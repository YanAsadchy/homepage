## Summary

For more than 8 years I have been working as a researcher and product designer on digital solutions across tech, healthcare, legal, and cultural sectors.

At **Generait AI Solutions** (Dublin, Aug 2023 – Sep 2025), I served as Head of Design. I designed a Microsoft Office legal-tech add-in that streamlined workflows by 20% and enabled €800 per seat per week in billable time savings. I led the design system and contributed to ISO 27001:2022 certification of the team.

At **MIT Media Lab** (Cambridge, Oct 2022 – Apr 2023), I applied large-scale analysis of cellphone mobility and geospatial data to study post-COVID cultural consumption recovery — research intended to inform evidence-based policies for recovery from global shocks.

In the health sector, at the **University of Oulu** (Apr 2022 – Jan 2023), I designed and deployed a gamified health application now used in participating hospitals across Europe for multiple sclerosis patients.

At **CUDAN Open Lab** (Tallinn, Sep 2019 – Jun 2023), I worked in the €2.5 million Horizon 2020 project combining machine learning and data visualisation to research digital culture, while teaching network science and ethnographic methods.

As founder of **Affinity OU** (est. May 2021, Tallinn), I manage a cross-functional teams and full product lifecycles from discovery to delivery.

## Motivations

I am inspired by the underlying laws of nature and how order emerges from chaos. My research interests focus on human interactions across domains: social networks, cultural consumption, city navigation, and platform behaviour.
