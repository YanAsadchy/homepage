## Summary of my experience

For more than 8 years I have been working as a researcher and a product designer on digital solutions across tech, healthcare, legal, and cultural sectors.

At Generait, I designed a Microsoft Office legal-tech add-in that streamlined workflows by 20% and enabled €800/seat/week in billable time savings. At **MIT Media Lab**, I applied big data analysis of cellphone and geospatial data to analyse post-COVID cultural consumption recovery and potentially inform policies for recovery from global shocks.

In the health sector, I designed and deployed a gamified health app now used in selected participating hospitals across Europe.

In academia, I combined machine learning and data visualisation to research digital behaviour while teaching network science and ethnographic methods.

As founder, I managed a cross-functional team and full product lifecycles, from discovery to delivery. I am experienced in **stakeholder engagement**, **systems thinking**, and translating complex requirements into user-centric design.

## I am constantly working in the following directions

- **Product Design**: End-to-end product design from discovery through delivery
- **UX Research**: User interviews, usability testing, A/B testing, data analysis
- **Design Systems**: Building and maintaining scalable design systems
- **Data Visualisation**: Making complex data readable and insightful
- **Leadership**: Cross-functional team management, stakeholder alignment
- **Research**: Academic research methodology, machine learning, statistical analysis

## What motivates me

I truly inspired by the nature, underlying laws and how order emerges from the chaos. Mainly I am interested in human interactions in various domains of life: social networks, cultural consumption, city navigation and more.
