I am a researcher, designer, and educator with a special interest in human nature, behaviour, and social media. Most of my time is spent on <Tooltip term="Content Analysis" description="Researching and studying aspects of objects relating to their meaning, such as identifying concepts or meaningful units in cultural and social media content." /> of cultural data (such as Social Media texts and images, archival records), and teaching courses on Design Systems, <Tooltip term="Data Visualisation" description="Creation and study of the visual representation of data." />, and AI.

I am the founder of [Affinity OU](https://affinity.ee), a Research and Development agency that creates custom web and mobile applications for startups and research institutions searching for a new ways of interacting with internet and archival data.

This website collects information about me, my work, and my courses. If you want to collaborate, I prefer a phone call or an in-person conversation — though email or LinkedIn also work well.
