I am a researcher, designer, and educator with a special interest in human nature, behaviour, and social media. Most of my time is spent analysing cultural data and teaching courses on Design Systems, Data Visualisation, and AI.

I am the founder of [Affinity OU](https://yanasadchy.com/contacts), a Research and Development agency that creates custom web and mobile applications for startups and research institutions.

This website collects information about me, my work, and my courses. If you want to collaborate, I prefer a phone call or an in-person conversation — though email or LinkedIn also work well.
